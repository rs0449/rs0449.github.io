The Perfect Recipie: Heart-Shaped Chocolate-Chip Chocolate

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of butter, softened
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 1 cup of packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp of baking soda
  • 2 tsp of hot water
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 2 cups of semisweet choclate chips
  • 1 cup of chopped walnuts(optional)
  • 2 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 3 cups of all-purpose flour
  • heart shaped cookie cutter

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans. Cut cookies with the heart shaped cookie cutter.
  3. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
